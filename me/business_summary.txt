EduZen Agency - Business Summary

Company Overview:
EduZen is an innovative education-focused agency that provides flexible, community-driven educational services with a unique pay-when-you-succeed model.

Leadership:
- Ahmad Mustapha - BDFL (Benevolent Dictator For Life)

Services Offered:

1. Student-Teacher Matching (Grades 1-12)
   - Platform: WhatsApp group "telmiz w istez"
   - Target: Grade 1-12 students
   - Cost: Free for students
   - Revenue: Teachers pay 20% of their first paycheck upon successful matching
   - Required Information: Name, email, language, subjects, grade, location

2. University Student-Teacher Matching
   - Platform: WhatsApp group "taleb w istez"
   - Target: University students
   - Cost: Free for students
   - Revenue: Teachers pay 20% of their first paycheck upon successful matching
   - Required Information: Name, email, language, subjects, university level, location

3. Educational Program Advertisement
   - Platform: WhatsApp group "motadareb w khabeer"
   - Service: Advertising workshops, bootcamps, and educational programs
   - Revenue: 10% commission from program providers for each registered attendee

4. Language Teaching Services
   - Languages: English, Arabic, German, French, Spanish
   - Features: Flexible hours, expert language instructors
   - Target: All levels and age groups

5. International University Applications
   - Service: Application assistance for international universities
   - Pricing: Flat fees
   - Payment: Only upon acceptance

Unique Value Proposition:
- Flexibility in service delivery
- Pay-only-when-you-get-paid model
- Community-driven approach
- Success-based pricing structure

Mission:
To democratize access to quality education by connecting students with qualified teachers and educational opportunities through an accessible, community-based platform.

Vision:
To become the leading educational matchmaking and services platform in the region, fostering educational excellence through innovative, flexible solutions.
